About Medical Features

The feature analysis uses 4 key medical features that are extracted from medical imaging and diagnostic tests:

  • Radius Mean

    Measures the average size of the core tumor, indicating the overall size of the affected area.

  • Texture Mean

    Represents the standard deviation of gray-scale values, indicating the texture of the tumor surface.

  • Perimeter Mean

    Measures the perimeter of the core tumor, helping to understand the tumor's shape and spread.

  • Concavity Mean

    Indicates the severity of concave portions of the contour, which can be a sign of tumor invasiveness.

Please ensure all feature values are accurate and obtained from proper medical tests. Incorrect values may lead to inaccurate analysis results.
